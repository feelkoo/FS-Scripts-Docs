# FS Multijob

Advanced multi-job system with Discord integration and on-duty management.

- Allows players to hold multiple jobs simultaneously (configurable limit).
- Switch between active jobs through NUI with cooldown protection.
- Discord role sync automatically assigns/removes jobs based on roles.
- On-duty system with location-based toggle points and visual markers.
- Job history tracking, statistics, and admin management tools.
- Supports both ESX and QBCore frameworks.
